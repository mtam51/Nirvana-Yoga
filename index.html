We offer accessible classes to all, whether you are a beginner or more experienced and we organize weekly community activities that are healthy, challenging and fun!

Our Instructors

Anna Scott

Anna’s holistic and inspirational approach to Yoga brings a unique energy to Nirvana. As an avid traveler, Anna is a student of the Krishamacharya Yoga lineage and brings this ancient knowledge to our students. As an ambassador and as a follower of Yoga, Anna ensures that all classes are true and follows the righteous path.

Jen Sidney

Jen brings transformation and positive energy to our classes. With 8 years of Yoga experience Jen is guided by inspired actions and brings a calming wave to our sessions. She is an expert in Hatha and Vinyasa forms of Yoga and is also a disciple of the Bikram path. A lifelong student and a motivated guider, she brings joy and serenity to all our students.

Andrew Barry

Andrew teaches Vinyasa forms of Yoga and approaches each class with meditation of the Vinyasa form. Realistic and down-to-earth, Andrew realizes the goals of transformation with hearty humor and has been a student of Yoga since 2012 after an inspirational journey to India. Andrew also hosts online sessions and is available every Sunday also.
